FAQ

A few quick answers.

How is pricing calculated?

At checkout you see the total landed cost in your currency, including taxes and fees. The breakdown is shown before you confirm. No surprises.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your payment is held in escrow with a licensed payment service provider. It is only released once you confirm receipt of the parcel at handoff.
