How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

No sellers, no listings, no markups you can't explain. You pick the product, we match a traveller already flying your route, you confirm at handoff.

01

Order

Pick what you want from a store abroad. WERQA shows the landed cost in your local currency. The breakdown is shown before you confirm.

02

Match

A verified traveller already flying that route accepts and carries it as personal baggage. Your funds sit in escrow.

03

Handoff

Meet at the agreed point. Confirm receipt with photo and a one-time code. Money is released from escrow.

Built for trust

Designed so neither side has to take it on faith.

Money in escrow

Your payment is held safely until you confirm receipt at handoff. No release without confirmation.

Verified travellers

Every traveller passes identity verification and sanctions screening before they accept any order.

Photo + OTP at handoff

Both sides confirm at the meeting point: photo of the parcel, one-time code from you. On the record.

FAQ

A few quick answers.

How is pricing calculated?

At checkout you see the total landed cost in your currency, including taxes and fees. The breakdown is shown before you confirm. No surprises.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your payment is held in escrow with a licensed payment service provider. It is only released once you confirm receipt at handoff.
