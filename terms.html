2. Definitions

  • "Shopper" — a registered user who places an Order for a product to be purchased and delivered by a Traveler.
  • "Traveler" — a registered user who has completed enhanced identity verification and accepts Orders on a route they are already travelling.
  • "Order" — a request placed by a Shopper for a specific product, including item details, Corridor, value, weight, and delivery preferences.
  • "Corridor" — a specific origin-to-destination route. The Day-1 active Corridor is UAE ↔ Pakistan.
  • "Service Fee" — a 4% fee charged by WERQA to the Shopper on the item value.
  • "Traveler Fee" — calculated on a sliding scale: 15% for items up to AED 500, 12% for AED 500–1,500, 8% above AED 1,500, with a floor of AED 40 per trip. The Traveler receives 80% of the Traveler Fee; WERQA retains a 20% platform share.
  • "Platform Fee" — a flat AED 22 per Order.
  • "Buyer Protection Fee" — an optional 4% of item value Shopper opt-in fee.
  • "Last-Mile Delivery Fee" — fee for using a delivery partner instead of in-person handoff. Disclosed at checkout.
  • "FX Spread" — 0.8–1.0% Day 1; embedded in the rate shown at checkout.
  • "Traveler Bond" — a one-time AED 200 refundable deposit posted by a Traveler before accepting their first Order.
  • "Secure Payment Holding (Escrow)" — the process by which WERQA holds the Shopper's payment until delivery is confirmed.

5. How the Platform works

Product Discovery. A Shopper may discover products by browsing the WERQA Product Catalog or submitting any product URL from a supported online retailer. The Platform calculates total landed cost in the Shopper's local currency.

Order Placement. The Shopper submits an Order. The Compliance Engine runs an automated check against destination-Corridor customs rules. Orders flagged as prohibited are blocked.

Price Tolerance. If actual purchase price differs from estimate by 15% or less, the Traveler may proceed. Above 15% requires Shopper re-confirmation.

Matching. The Platform matches the Order with an available verified Traveler whose Trip route, dates, and bag capacity fit.

Purchase by Traveler. The Traveler purchases the item using their own personal funds.

Ownership and Transfer. Upon delivery and confirmation, the Traveler is engaging in a private resale of personal property to the Shopper, facilitated through a peer-to-peer transaction mechanism provided by the Platform. WERQA is a technology platform and is not a party to the transaction between shopper and traveler.

Payment Release. Upon confirmed delivery, WERQA releases the Shopper's held payment: (a) item purchase price reimbursed to Traveler; (b) 80% of the Traveler Fee paid to Traveler; (c) WERQA retains the Service Fee, Platform Fee, FX Spread, Last-Mile margin, optional Buyer Protection Fee, and the 20% WERQA share of the Traveler Fee.

Delivery Modes:

  • Direct meetup. Public meeting point. Shopper home address never shared with Traveler.
  • Partner Drop-off. Traveler hands parcel to a WERQA-authorised local delivery partner; partner completes last-mile delivery.
  • Partner Pickup. Parcel left at a WERQA-authorised collection point; Shopper collects with OTP + ID verification.

7. Secure Payment Holding (Escrow)

When a Shopper places an Order, payment is authorised and held until delivery is confirmed. Payment is released when the Traveler marks delivery with photo evidence AND the Shopper confirms receipt, or 72 hours pass without dispute.

8. Traveler obligations

Travelers are independent contractors, not employees, agents, or representatives of WERQA.

Travelers acknowledge that they purchase items using their own personal funds and assume the financial risk until delivery is confirmed and payment is released.

8.5 Traveler Bond

  • Amount: AED 200, posted once before accepting the first Order.
  • Nature: refundable deposit; not a Platform fee.
  • Refund: returned on (i) successful completion of three Orders; or (ii) account closure in good standing.
  • Forfeit triggers: verified no-show, material misrepresentation in KYC, or proven failure to deliver an accepted Order without reasonable cause.
  • Forfeit split: 50% to the affected Shopper, 50% to WERQA.

WERQA never moves money outbound on a Traveler's behalf. Traveler payouts are credited to the Traveler's UAE-controlled account.
