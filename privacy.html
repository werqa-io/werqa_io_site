Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Insert before publication] · Last Updated: 28 April 2026 · Data Controller: WERQA, Abu Dhabi, UAE · Contact: info@werqa.io

This Privacy Policy describes how WERQA collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data, in compliance with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data ("UAE PDPL"), and where applicable Pakistan's Personal Data Protection regime and EU GDPR.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Account information

Full legal name, email address, phone number, nationality, date of birth.

1.2 Identity verification data (Travelers)

Government-issued ID document scan, biometric liveness data, facial recognition data, AML/sanctions screening results. Processed by our verification partner, Sumsub, in their EU-based data centers.

1.3 Transaction data

Order details, item descriptions, payment amounts, delivery addresses, corridor information, order status history, dispute records. Payment card data is processed directly by our payment gateway partner, PayTabs, and is never accessed or stored by WERQA.

1.4 Travel data (Travelers)

Flight booking confirmations, departure/arrival dates, route information, flight numbers.

1.5 Usage data

App usage analytics, device information, IP address, browser type, pages visited, features used, crash reports.

1.6 Communication data

In-app messages between Shoppers and Travelers, support ticket content, feedback submissions.

1.7 Location data

Approximate location (city-level) for corridor matching. We do not track real-time GPS location.

2. Legal basis for processing

Under UAE PDPL, we process your data based on:

  • Contract performance — necessary to provide our marketplace services.
  • Legitimate interest — analytics, fraud prevention, platform improvement.
  • Legal obligation — AML screening, regulatory compliance, tax records.
  • Consent — marketing communications (you may withdraw consent at any time).

3. How we use your data

  • Facilitating marketplace transactions between Shoppers and Travelers.
  • Identity verification and fraud prevention.
  • Compliance checking against corridor customs regulations.
  • Processing payments through our gateway partner.
  • Resolving disputes between users.
  • Sending transactional notifications (order updates, delivery confirmations).
  • Improving Platform functionality and user experience.
  • Marketing communications (with consent only).
  • Regulatory compliance and legal obligations.

5. Data retention

  • Transaction records — 24 months after last activity (regulatory and dispute resolution).
  • KYC documents — 6 months after user account closure.
  • Support communications — 30 days after ticket resolution.
  • Audit logs — 12 months (security and regulatory).
  • Marketing consent records — until withdrawn.

6. Your rights

Under UAE PDPL and equivalent regimes, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to erasure").
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@werqa.io. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard security measures:

  • TLS 1.3 in transit; encryption at rest for sensitive PII.
  • Authentication tokens stored in OS-level secure storage on mobile devices (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore).
  • Row-level security on every table in our database; admin actions audited.
  • HMAC-SHA256 signature validation on all third-party webhooks.
  • Annual penetration testing; dependency scanning on every release.

8. International data transfers

Your data may be transferred to data centers outside your country of residence (e.g., EU, UAE, Saudi Arabia) for processing. All transfers comply with UAE PDPL and rely on appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses and country adequacy decisions where available.

9. Children's privacy

WERQA is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have such data, contact us immediately at info@werqa.io.

10. Cookies and trackers

The WERQA mobile app and website use only essential cookies/storage required for authentication and core functionality. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. Analytics (PostHog, Sentry) are opt-in on first app launch.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated to users at least 14 days before taking effect via email and in-app notification.
